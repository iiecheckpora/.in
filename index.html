About Our Institute

"IIE is Islamic research, training, and solution-providing non-profit Institute, established with the objective to help people in acquiring knowledge of the Quran and Sunnah upon the Understanding of Salaf-Us-Saalih. Our goal and mission is to call to the path of Allah in worshiping him alone (Tawheed) and following the guidance of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.Our institution is established on the principles of Ahlus Sunnah Wa Al Jamaah. These guiding principles are derived from the correct understanding of the Quran and the Sunnah upon the understanding of the pious predecessors (Salaf as Salih). The foundation of this true methodology sprinkles from the statement of Allah in the Quran in Surah At-Tawbah, Ayah 100"
